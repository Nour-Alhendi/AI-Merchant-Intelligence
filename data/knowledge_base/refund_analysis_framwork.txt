Refund Analysis Framework

Refund ratio = number of refunds / total transactions.

Typical healthy refund ratios:
- Low risk: below 10%
- Medium risk: 10%â€“30%

High refund ratios may indicate:

Refund patterns should be analyzed alongside chargebacks and transaction spikes.
